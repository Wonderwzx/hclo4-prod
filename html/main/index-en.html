About Us

COMPANY PROFILE

Tianjin Xinyuan Chemical Co., Ltd., established in 1995, specializes in the production of Perchloric acid and Perchlorate (Lithium perchlorate and Magnesium perchlorate), which are inorganic fine chemical products.

COMPANY ADVANTAGES

With 30 years of production experience, all products meet export standards, with an annual output of 9900 tons. Our factory has introduced testing equipment from Jena, Germany, and the testing accuracy exceeds that of medium-sized testing institutions.

INSPECTION EQUIPMENT

Our company has a standardized inspection process, detection equipment in Jena, Germany, and Inductively coupled plasma mass spectrometer, and the detection accuracy has been improved to the ppb level.
